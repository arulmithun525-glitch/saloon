Our Story

British Heritage,
Indian Heart

Founded in London in 1963 by brothers Toni and Guy Mascolo, TONI&GUY has become one of the world's most iconic hair fashion brands, operating in 48 countries and over 480 salons worldwide.

In India, we bring decades of British hairdressing expertise to over 120 premium salons. Our mission is simple: to deliver world-class hair artistry with an understanding of local style sensibilities.

Education has always been at the heart of everything we do. Every TONI&GUY stylist undergoes rigorous, internationally certified training to ensure you receive the best possible service, every time.
